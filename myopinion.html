My opinion

What type of business should you start?

In my opinion, the type of business you should start depends on many factors, including how much money you have to start, your expectations for the business, and the amount of time your able to put into it.

A project for fun

If you just want to start a small business as more of a test or just a fun idea, I think you should try an online business. You could create a simple one using Shopify and market it for free on social media. This could easily cost you under $100 and would be a fun experiment.

A side hustle

If you want to start a small side hustle, a great option would be a vending machine or a small laundromat business. These businesses practically run themselves, allowing you to stop by once a week to fill up and collect money. While these wouldn't replace your normal job, they would be a great way to add an extra income stream. You would be able to use the profits to go on a vacation, help pay the bills, or put it towards a larger business.

Full time

If you have the time to fully devote yourself to your business, then I would recommend starting a simple service based business. If you chose something simple that you could do, then you don't have to hire any employees to begin with, and you might not need to get many permits. Business could be slow at first, but if you start something like a lawn mowing business, then you could get repeat customers, allowing for a steady income stream and potentially expanding your business to a multi person operation.
